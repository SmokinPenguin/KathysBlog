DEBS

9/10

I really like the plot of this movie. The special effects were not great, but I will overlook that. At least they tried. I like the characters and the storyline. It is homophobic that everyone walked in on the sex scene with Amy and Lucy Diamond. Why did Lucy not have a lock on the door? They couldn’t have waited another day to find Amy? It had to be right when they were having sex. The lesbians can’t enjoy anything!
