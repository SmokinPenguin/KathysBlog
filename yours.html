Yours Cruelly, Elvira Memoirs of the Mistress of the Dark

9/10

This is an amazing memoir. I know so much Elvria trivia now. Cassandra Peterson has been through a lot. I’m grateful that she shared her life story. There needs to be a sexual assault trigger warning though. I learned about show business and Cassandra’s life. This is a great read.
